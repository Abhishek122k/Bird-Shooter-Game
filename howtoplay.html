HOW TO PLAY

1. Single-player shooter game with 5 lives and you will start with 1 point initially.

2. Shoot down passing objects to increase your score by 1; missing decreases your lives by 1.

3. Score checkpoints: Every 20 points and if you have 5 lives earns you 10 points.

4. Every 40 points, you gain 1 life.
