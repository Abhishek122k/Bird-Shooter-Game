HOW TO PLAY

1. Single-player shooter game with 5 lives.

2. Shoot down passing objects to increase your score by 1; missing decreases your lives by 1.

3. Score checkpoints: Every 15 points earn you 1 life, capped at 5 lives.

4. Every 25 points, a 10-second powerup grants immunity from losing lives.

5. Objective: Achieve the highest score by shooting objects and strategically using powerups.
